Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

  • 1. How does the random dinner generator work?

    • What to Eat?? randomly selects an ethnic flavor profile, a protein, and a starch to create a unique dinner suggestion. It then recommends a recipe based on the selected combination.

  • 2. Why does the app ask for my location?

    • The app requests access to your location to suggest nearby restaurants that match the flavor profile you've selected. Your location data is only used for this purpose and is not stored or shared.

  • 3. How can I upgrade to the Pro version?

    • You can upgrade to What to Eat?? Pro by purchasing the Pro version in the Apple App Store. The Pro version unlocks additional features such as more flavor profiles, advanced recipe search options, and an ad-free experience.

  • 4. The app isn't functioning properly, what should I do?

    • If the app isn't working as expected, try restarting the app or your device. If the issue persists, please contact our support team using the information below.
