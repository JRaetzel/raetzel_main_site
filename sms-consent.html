SMS Consent

This page explains how users may voluntarily opt in to receive SMS messages from Raetzel Resolutions LLC.

SMS messaging is optional. Consent to receive SMS messages is not required to create an account, use our services, purchase products, or complete any transaction.

Example Account Settings Opt-In

After an account has been created, users may choose to enable SMS notifications from within their account settings. SMS notifications are disabled by default and must be actively enabled by the user.

Notification Settings

By checking this box, I agree to receive SMS notifications from Raetzel Resolutions LLC. Message frequency may vary. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP to opt out or HELP for assistance. Consent is not a condition of purchase or use of services.

Other Opt-In Methods

Users may also opt in by texting JOIN or SUBSCRIBE to the Raetzel Resolutions LLC messaging number, or by providing verbal consent directly to a Raetzel Resolutions LLC representative.

Opt-Out and Help

Users may opt out at any time by replying STOP. Users may request assistance by replying HELP.
