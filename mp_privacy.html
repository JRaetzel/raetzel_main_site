Thank you for choosing Math Parent 101. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it through our compliance with this Privacy Policy. This policy describes how we handle any personal information collected by our app and the permissions required for its functionality.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

Math Parent 101 does not collect, store, or share any personal information. We do not require you to provide any personal data to use the app, and we do not track, monitor, or sell any information related to your use of the app.

2. Permissions Required

Math Parent 101 does not require any permissions from your device. Everything is kept local in the app.

3. Third-Party Services

Math Parent 101 does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The app is designed to respect your privacy by not integrating with any external data collection services.

4. Data Security

Since Math Parent 101 does not collect or store any personal data, there are no specific data security measures required. The app operates entirely on your device and does not transmit data to external servers.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the app and will be effective as of the date of the update. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your privacy.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or the privacy practices of Math Parent 101, please contact us at support@raetzel.org.
