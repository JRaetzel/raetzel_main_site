Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

  • 1. What is Math Parent 101?

    • Math Parent 101 is a math helper app designed specifically for parents who want to check or understand their child’s math homework. It provides commonly used math formulas, step-by-step solutions, and final answers for clarity and learning support.

  • 2. What grade levels is this app for?

    • The app is designed to support upper elementary through high school math. It covers many formulas commonly used in grades 4–12, including geometry, algebra, and graphing concepts.

  • 3. Can students use the app too?

    • While the app is designed for parents, students can also use it under parental guidance. To encourage responsible use, users must solve a basic math problem before accessing the full app.

  • 4. What kind of math topics are included?

    • The app includes a variety of topics like: • Area and volume formulas • Pythagorean theorem • Slope and distance formulas • Surface area • Graphing and equations • FOIL method for multiplying polynomials • And more!

  • 5. How do I enter exponents like x² or x³?

    • You can type them in as x^2, x^3, or use shortcuts like xsq for x². The app automatically recognizes and converts these formats.

  • 6. How do I dismiss the keyboard after typing?

    • Tap the keyboard dismiss button (a small keyboard icon with a down arrow) usually located in the bottom-right of the screen. In some text fields, a “Done” button may also appear above the keyboard.

  • 7. Can I copy the answers or steps from the app?

    • No, we do not allow selecting or copying text from the app to help deter irresponsible use of the app.

  • 8. Will more formulas be added in the future??

    • Absolutely! We plan to continue expanding the app based on user feedback. If there’s a formula you’d like to see, feel free to contact us.

  • 9. Is internet required to use the app?

    • Nope! Most of the app works completely offline, so you can access formulas and solutions without Wi-Fi or data.

  • 10. I found a bug / have a suggestion — how do I contact support?

    • Please email us at [your support email here]. We love hearing from users and are always working to make Math Parent 101 better!
